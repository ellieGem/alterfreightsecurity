The standard business conditions below, referred to as TSTC, shall apply to all services conducted, performed or provided by the company operating as freight forwarders including in in case this company issues a bill of lading or another document proving the contract of carriage between a party other than the company and the customer or shipper.

2. In the case of a company acting as a carrier, the provisions of the documents issued by or on behalf of the company bearing the bill of lading, whether negotiable or not, shall be mandatory and in In case of conflict of conditions, the provisions of such documents will still cover the conditions of TSTC within the scope of conflict.
